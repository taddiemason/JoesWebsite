This domain name has been seized by the United States Secret Service (USSS), pursuant to a seizure warrant issued by a United States District Court under the authority of 18 U.S.C. §§ 981 and 2323.

This action was taken in connection with an ongoing criminal investigation involving the unauthorized impersonation of individuals and public figures, in violation of federal law. The use of false identities to deceive, defraud, or mislead others is a serious offense under 18 U.S.C. § 912 and related statutes.

Engaging in character impersonation, financial fraud, identity theft, and other cyber-enabled crimes may result in criminal prosecution, with penalties including up to 30 years in federal prison, fines exceeding $1,000,000, and the forfeiture of assets obtained through illegal conduct. (18 U.S.C. §§ 1028, 1029, 1343, 1344)

The United States Secret Service remains committed to protecting the public and preserving the integrity of online communications by investigating and prosecuting individuals who exploit the anonymity of the internet to commit criminal acts.
