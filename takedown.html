THIS DOMAIN HAS BEEN SEIZED

This domain name has been seized by the United States Secret Service (USSS), pursuant to a seizure warrant issued by a United States District Court under the authority of 18 U.S.C. §§ 981 and 2323.

Engaging in financial fraud, identity theft, and other cyber-enabled crimes is a violation of federal law. Violators may face penalties including up to 30 years in federal prison, fines exceeding $1,000,000, and the forfeiture of assets gained through illegal activity. (18 U.S.C. §§ 1028, 1029, 1343, 1344)

The United States Secret Service will continue to aggressively investigate and prosecute those who target the integrity of our financial and communications systems.
